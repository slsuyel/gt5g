about us

GT-5G is a company building 5G base stations under the [Qualcomm] roof. The basic technologies we invent, and share the inventions with the entire mobile industry through the technology licensing model, and become a strong backing for customers and partners to innovate, compete and develop globally. Beginning in 1995, this model is the foundation of Qualcomm's core mission, values ​​and culture, driving the vigorous development of the market and continuing to benefit consumers.
